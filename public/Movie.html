The Best Movies According to Nikhat

My Top 3 Movies of All-time.

Taare Zameen Par

This film showcases a teacher's extraordinary efforts to understand and nurture a dyslexic child's unique talents, highlighting the transformative power of empathetic teaching..

Hichki

The film not only reminds us of the significant role of our teachers in our lives, but also echoes the truth that perseverance can help you rise above any difficulty.

3 idiots

Chase your dreams, work hard and run through the system and achieve them.
